Aptitude

The Aptitude Round is the first elimination stage in placements, assessing problem-solving, reasoning, and math skills. It covers Quantitative Aptitude , Logical Reasoning , Verbal Ability and Data Interpretation. Career Companion helps with frequently asked questions, step-by-step solutions, and mock tests to improve speed and accuracy.

Coding

The Coding Round tests problem-solving and programming skills, focusing on data structures, algorithms, and logical thinking. Common topics include arrays, linked lists, recursion, sorting, searching, dynamic programming, and OOP concepts. Candidates may be required to solve coding problems in languages like C, C++, Java, or Python.Career Companion provides company-specific coding questions, optimized solutions, and mock coding tests to enhance preparation.

Interview

The Interview Round evaluates a candidate’s technical knowledge, problem-solving ability, and communication skills. It includes technical interviews and HR interviews. Questions may range from coding challenges and system design to behavioral and situational responses. Career Companion offers frequently asked interview questions, expert answers, and mock interview practice to boost confidence.

Online Tests

Online tests help candidates prepare effectively for Aptitude, Coding, and Interview rounds by simulating real exam conditions. These tests cover quantitative aptitude, logical reasoning, verbal ability, coding problems, and technical MCQs. Practicing online enhances speed, accuracy, and problem-solving skills. Career Companion provides company-specific mock tests, timed practice sessions, and detailed explanations to help students excel in placements.

Resume Building

Resume Building is crucial for placements, highlighting skills, projects, and achievements. Career Companion helps create professional, ATS-friendly resumes tailored to company requirements, ensuring a strong first impression.
